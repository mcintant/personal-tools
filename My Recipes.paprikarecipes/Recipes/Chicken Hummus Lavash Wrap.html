Chicken Hummus Lavash Wrap

Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: Yields 6-8 wraps Source: Cookwell.com

Ingredients

red pepper flakes SPICY

pepperoncini peppers , thinly sliced SPICY

lettuce , thinly sliced CRISPY

red onions , thinly sliced PUNGENT

Directions

Cook the chicken

Thin out the chicken breasts to an even thickness.

Sprinkle each breast with some salt.

Then, grill or sear the butterflied (thinned out) chicken breasts in a hot pan until they reach 155°F internal temperature. Set aside to cool, then slice the chicken into long thin strips.

Make the white sauce

Combine mayo, low-fat sour cream, za'atar, red pepper flakes, and lemon juice. Taste and adjust as needed.

EAT.
