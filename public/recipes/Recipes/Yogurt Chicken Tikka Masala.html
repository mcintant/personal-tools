Yogurt Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

Marinade

1 cup plain full-fat greek yogurt (5% fage)

2 tablespoons good tandoori masala (spice walla in video)

2 teaspoons kosher salt

2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs


Yogurt Naan

1 cup plain full-fat greek yogurt (5% fage)

1 1/3 cup self rising flour

1/2 tsp kosher salt

Description

Total Recipe Cost (4 servings) $9.98

Cost Per Serving $2.50

Directions


1. Make the marinade and place in ziplock bag
2. Place chicken
2. Marinate chicken overnight (or in fridge for a couple hours)

1. Make the marinade and place in ziplock bag
2. Place chicken in ziplock bag and mush around
3. Marinate chicken overnight (or in fridge for a couple hours)

Set oven to broil on high
Bake on foil (will have a ton of burnt bits if not!!)
Lay thighs out in one even layer and slide into top rack
Once every piece has black speckling take out, flip each piece over and return until the broiler until the other side gets to the same appearance.
15 minutes in total

Yogurt Naan
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, and knead for 5 minutes until cohesive and strong
Dive into 8 pieces before rolling out and cooking over high heat
Cook with ghee
￼
only needs a few seconds to brown

Yogurt sauce 1/4 cup full fat plain yogurt
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 lemon juiced
1 tsp chopped cilantro
